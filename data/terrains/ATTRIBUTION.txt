The raw data for all maps, unless noted otherwise, comes from the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration's website:

    https://maps.ngdc.noaa.gov/viewers/wcs-client/
    
The files Iceland.terrain, RioDeJaneiro.terrain, PearlRiverDelta.terrain, TeIkaAMaui.terrain, VelingalaSenegal.terrain, and GulfOfGuinea.terrain were derived from the GEBCO 2020 Gridded Bathymetry Survey:

    https://download.gebco.net/
    
Attribution is as follows:

    "GEBCO Compilation Group (2020) GEBCO 2020 Grid (doi:10.5285/a29c5465-b138-234d-e053-6c86abc040b9)."

Some of the data were smoothed using a two-step grid refinement algorithm followed by a Gaussian blur.
